MOMOH Mobile Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2024

1. Introduction

MOMOH Mobile ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide:

  • Account information (email address, name)
  • Restaurant and location data
  • Form submissions and responses
  • User preferences and settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage statistics
  • Login timestamps
  • Error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Process and manage form submissions
  • Improve user experience
  • Send important notifications
  • Monitor app performance
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure data encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data transmission

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Firebase (for authentication and data storage)
  • Analytics providers (to improve app performance)
  • Your restaurant organization (for operational purposes)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection
  • Export your data

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request data deletion by contacting us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
